# Wrong Questions Analysis

## Q32

**Question**

According to the speaker, some scientists believe that nano-particles:

A. should be restricted to secure environments  
B. should be used with more caution  
C. should only be developed for essential products  

**My Answer**

A

**Correct Answer**

B

**Transcript**

greater care should be taken with nano particles in laboratories and factories

**Error Type**

Synonym recognition

**Explanation**

The speaker says **greater care**, which corresponds to **more caution**.

| Transcript | Option |
|-------------|--------|
greater care | more caution |

Option A is misleading because the lecture does not say nano-particles should only be used in secure environments.

**Fix**

Practice synonym mapping:
care → caution
risk → danger
issue → problem

## Q34

**Question**

Nanotechnology could allow the development of stronger ______.

**My Answer**

transportation

**Correct Answer**

metals

**Transcript**

Thanks to nanotechnology, there could be a major breakthrough in the field of transportation with the production of more durable metals.

**Error Type**

Filled answer too early

**Explanation**

The keyword **transportation** appeared earlier but was not the answer.

The answer comes later:
production of more durable metals


**Fix**

Do not fill answers before the complete noun phrase appears.

## Q37

**Question**

______ energy will become more affordable.

**My Answer**

scenary

**Correct Answer**

solar

**Transcript**

The cost of solar cells will be drastically reduced.

**Error Type**

Vocabulary recognition

**Explanation**

The speaker mentions **solar cells**, so the correct answer is **solar energy**.

**Fix**

Review common energy vocabulary:
solar
wind
nuclear
renewable
power
energy

## Q40

**Question**

Analysis of medical ______ will be speeded up.

**My Answer**

test

**Correct Answer**

tests

**Transcript**

biosensors are developed to analyse tests in minutes rather than days

**Error Type**

Singular / plural error

**Explanation**

The structure:
analysis of ______


usually requires a **plural noun**.
Examples:
analysis of samples
analysis of tests
analysis of results


**Fix**
Before listening, predict grammar:
noun (plural)
