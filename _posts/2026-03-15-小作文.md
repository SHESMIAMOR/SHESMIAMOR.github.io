## 🔍 关键问题（最影响提分的重点）

### 1）**单位与数字读取错误（最致命）**
图表纵轴是 **metric tonnes (thousands)**，所以：
- 图上 **160** 代表 **160,000** 吨（约数）

你文中这一句必须修正：
- 你写：**“By 2000, … NO2 … risen to 15,000 metric tonnes …”** ❌  
- 更合理：**NO2 在 2000 年约为 160,000 metric tonnes** ✅

> Task 1 允许 **about/around** 的近似，但不接受**数量级错误**。

---

### 2）**高频拼写/术语错误（直接扣分）**
- **ozone hold → ozone hole**  
- **H202 → H2O2**  
- **growed → grew**  
- “in Antarctic” 更自然：**over Antarctica / in the Antarctic**

---

### 3）**Overall 概括需要更精确（写出对比关系）**
你写：**“most harmful gases increased, except Freon.”**（不够精确）  
更像高分的总览应该突出：
- **Freon 先升后降（mid-1980s peak → sharp fall）**
- **NO2 和 H2O2 总体上升（尤其 1990 后）**

## ✍️ 改写范文（Band 7 方向｜重点已加粗）

The line graphs show changes in the **size of the Antarctic ozone hole** and the **production of three ozone-damaging gases** (**Freon, NO2 and H2O2**) from 1980 to 2000.

**Overall, the ozone hole expanded dramatically**, with the **fastest growth between 1985 and 1990**. In contrast, **Freon peaked in 1985 and then fell sharply**, while **NO2 and H2O2 increased over time**, especially after 1990.

In 1980, the ozone hole measured **roughly 40–50 thousand square kilometres**. It then grew to **about 80 thousand** in 1985 and reached **nearly 200 thousand** by 1990. After that, the figure rose more gradually, to **just over 220 thousand** in 1995 and **around 235–240 thousand** in 2000.

Freon production stood at **about 150 thousand tonnes** in 1980 and climbed to **just over 200 thousand** in 1985, before dropping to **around 150 thousand** in 1990 and **roughly 20 thousand** by 2000. By contrast, NO2 increased from **around 100 thousand tonnes** in 1980 to **just above 100 thousand** in 1990, then climbed to **approximately 160 thousand** in 2000. H2O2 also rose steadily from **near zero** to **about 80 thousand tonnes** by the end of the period.
