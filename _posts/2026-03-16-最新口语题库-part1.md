# 2026年1-4月 雅思口语 Part 1 新题库汇总

## 1. Life stages (人生阶段)
1. What did you often do with your friends in your childhood?
   /n When we were very young, we'd play together in the community playground. However, our parents eventually got us tablets, so we'd played together online most of the time. But once in a while, we'd also play badminton together. That's the sport that appealed to us most when we were young.
   
2. What do you think is the most important at the moment?
   At the moment, graduating from university is my priority. I'm in my final year, and things are really hectic now. Though I've got many electives, which is good, I also have a thesis and two major projects to complete. So, my focus is to pass all requirements and graduate.
   
3. Do you have any plans for the next five years?
   Yes. I hope to be an animator in one of the big cartooning firms. That's why I'm taking the IELTS: so I can study for a master's in New Zealand or Australia to improve my credentials. I then plan to come back to my country to find a job in an animation studio.
   
4. How do people remember each stage of their lives?
   Most people have photo albums, usually digital, to view and remember their momentous occasions. I know of a few, however, who also do journaling. According to them, journaling allows them to write down their emotions about an event, making it a better record of what happened before.
   
5. Do you enjoy being the age you are now?
   Not really. I do love my life, but I just think my situation will be better once I'm employed and living independently.As a student, I get by on the allowance my parents send me, which is rarely ever enough. It's disappointing when I have to skip out on things because I don't have money. So I'm looking forward to the next chapter in my life.
   
6. At what age do you think people are the happiest?
   In my view, it's between 25 and 30 years old. I believe that people in that age range have the freedom and resources to do as they please. They can see the world, party all night, meet lots of new people, accomplish their dreams without worrying about big responsibilities, such as sending their kids to school or preparing for retirement.

## 2. Childhood activities (童年活动)
1. What are your favourite activities?
2. What were your favourite activities when you were a child?
3. Did you prefer to do activities alone or with a group of people when you were a child?
4. Are there any differences between the activities you liked when you were a child and those you like now?

## 3. Scenery (风景)
1. Do you look out the window at the scenery when travelling by bus or car?
2. Do you prefer the mountains or the sea?
3. Do you like to take scenery pictures?
4. What are the most beautiful sights you have seen while travelling?

## 4. Views (景色/景观)
1. Do you like taking pictures of different views?
2. Do you prefer views in urban areas or rural areas?
3. Do you prefer views in your own country or in other countries?
4. Have you seen an unforgettable and beautiful view or scenery?

## 5. Building (建筑)
1. Are there tall buildings near your home?
2. Do you take photos of buildings?
3. Is there a building that you would like to visit?
4. Do you want to live in a tall building?

## 6. Hobby (爱好)
1. Do you have any hobbies?
2. Did you have any hobbies when you were a child?
3. Do you have a hobby that you've had since childhood?
4. Do you have the same hobbies as your family members?

## 7. Gifts (礼物)
1. Have you ever sent handmade gifts to others?
2. Have you ever received a great gift?
3. What do you consider when choosing a gift?
4. Do you think you are good at choosing gifts?
5. What gift have you received recently?

## 8. Walking (步行)
1. Do you walk a lot?
2. Did you often go outside to have a walk when you were a child?
3. Why do people like to walk in parks?
4. Where would you like to take a long walk if you had the chance?
5. Where did you go for a walk lately?

## 9. Sports team (运动队)
1. Have you ever been part of a sports team?
2. Are team sports popular in your culture?
3. Do you like watching team games? Why?
4. What are the differences between team sports and individual sports?

## 10. Food (食物)
1. What is your favourite food?
2. What kind of food did you like when you were young?
3. Do you eat different foods at different times of the year?
4. Has your favourite food changed since you were a child?

## 11. Typing (打字)
1. Do you prefer typing or handwriting?
2. Do you type on a desktop or laptop keyboard every day?
3. When did you learn how to type on a keyboard?
4. How do you improve your typing?

## 12. Morning time (早晨时光)
1. Do you like getting up early in the morning?
2. What do you usually do in the morning?
3. What did you do in the morning when you were little? Why?
4. Are there any differences between what you do in the morning now and what you did in the past?
5. Do you spend your mornings doing the same things on both weekends and weekdays? Why?

## 13. Reading (阅读)
1. Do you like reading?
2. Do you prefer to read on paper or on a screen?
3. When do you need to read carefully, and when not?
4. Do you prefer scanning or detailed reading?

## 14. Dreams (梦)
1. Can you remember the dreams you had?
2. Do you share your dreams with others?
3. Do you think dreams have special meanings?
4. Do you want to make your dreams come true?

## 15. Pets and Animals (宠物与动物)
1. What's your favourite animal? Why?
2. Where do you prefer to keep your pet, indoors or outdoors?
3. Have you ever had a pet before?
4. What is the most popular animal in China?

## 16. Day off (休息日)
1. When was the last time you had a few days off?
2. What do you usually do when you have days off?
3. Do you usually spend your days off with your parents or with your friends?
4. What would you like to do if you had a day off tomorrow?
