# Wrong Question Analysis

## Q31

### Question
**figures revealing bones**

### My Answer
A Dynamic

### Correct Answer
C Modern

### Evidence from the transcript
> the Modern style, which is known as "x-ray" because it actually makes a feature of the **internal skeleton** as well as the organs of animals and humans

### Why I got it wrong
题干里的 **revealing bones** 对应原文里的 **internal skeleton**。

我没有抓住这个同义替换，反而被前面 Dynamic 风格的信息带跑了。

### Core paraphrase
- revealing bones = internal skeleton
- x-ray style = Modern

### Error Type
- Synonym recognition failure
- Style confusion

### Fix
以后看到这些词要立刻联想 **Modern**：

- bones
- skeleton
- organs
- x-ray

## Q34

### Question
**figures smaller than life size**

### My Answer
C Modern

### Correct Answer
A Dynamic

> the more recent images tend to be life size or even larger, but the **Dynamic figures are painted in miniature**

### Why I got it wrong
题干里的 **smaller than life size** 对应原文里的 **painted in miniature**。

我知道题目在说“尺寸”，但没有把 **miniature** 准确对应到 **smaller than life size**。

### Core paraphrase

### Error Type

# Error Pattern Summary

## Main Problems

### 1. I confused the three painting styles

### 2. I missed paraphrases
比如：

- revealing bones = internal skeleton

- smaller than life size = painted in miniature

这种 matching 题如果不边听边记分类，很容易串答案。
