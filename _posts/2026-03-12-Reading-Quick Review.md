# Core Mistakes

## 1. I swapped nearby answers

### Q29
- My answer: **economic**
- Correct: **farming**

### Q30
- My answer: **farming**
- Correct: **economic**

### Lesson
相邻空容易互换。  
以后 summary 连续填空时，要一起看：

- 局部内容
- 整体结果

---

## 2. I ignored grammar / word class

### Q32
- My answer: **immigrants**
- Correct: **generous**

Sentence:
> with ______ offer on land use

### Lesson
**offer** 前面要形容词。  
所以：

- generous offer ✅
- immigrants offer ❌

---

## 3. I chose a background-related word, not the exact one

### Q33
- My answer: **explorers**
- Correct: **immigrants**

### Lesson
原文说的是：

- settlers
- influx of people
- start a new life

这些都对应 **immigrants**，不是 explorers。

---

## 4. I missed very direct synonyms

### Q31
- My answer: **thriving**
- Correct: **bought**

Original:
> purchased Rupert's Land

### Lesson
- purchased = bought

这类同义替换很直接，不能错。

---

## 5. I interpreted metaphor literally

### Q39
- My answer: **A**
- Correct: **D**

Original:
> a huge demographic railway station, in which large numbers of people came and went, with departures often outnumbering arrivals

### Lesson
这里不是在说“交通方便”，而是在说：

- people came and went
- departures often outnumbered arrivals

所以真正含义是：

**population mobility + population loss**

# What This Passage Taught Me

## My main weakness here was:

- summary precision
- word class control
- exact synonym matching
- metaphor interpretation

## My strength here was:

- TRUE / FALSE / NOT GIVEN

这篇判断题全对，说明我不是整体阅读差，主要是 summary 和选择题精度不够。

# Next Time I Must Remember

## For summary completion:
1. 先看词性
2. 连续空一起判断
3. 选最直接的同义替换，不选大而泛的词

## For multiple choice:
1. 遇到比喻别按字面理解
2. 先问：作者真正想表达什么？
