## 2）引言高分模板

### 通用引言模板

```text
It is often argued that [题目改写]. While some people believe that [对立观点], I believe that [你的观点].
```

### 更自然一点的版本

```text
In recent years, there has been growing debate about whether [题目改写]. Although it is true that [对方观点], I would argue that [你的立场].
```

### 例子

题目：Some people think university education should be free for everyone.

你可以写：

```text
In recent years, there has been growing debate about whether university education should be free for all students. Although some people believe that individuals should pay for their own studies, I would argue that higher education ought to be made more affordable and accessible.
```

## 一篇 7–7.5 分范文

**Essay**

Access to clean water is widely regarded as a fundamental human right, and some people therefore argue that every household should receive water free of charge. While I agree that all people should be guaranteed access to a basic supply of safe water, I do not believe that all domestic water use ought to be provided entirely free.

On the one hand, there are strong reasons why governments should ensure that every home has access to clean water regardless of income. Water is essential for drinking, cooking, washing, and sanitation, and without it people face serious risks to both health and dignity. In this sense, access to a minimum amount of water is not a luxury but a necessity. Charging poor families for every drop they use could result in serious inequality, especially in low-income communities where people may already struggle to pay for other essentials. For this reason, providing a basic quantity of water free of charge is a reasonable and humane policy.

On the other hand, making all household water completely free could create significant problems. First, people tend to value resources less when they do not have to pay for them, which may lead to wasteful consumption. This is particularly concerning in regions where water is scarce. Second, water systems require enormous investment in purification, infrastructure, maintenance, and delivery. If users pay nothing at all, governments would have to bear the full financial burden, which may be unsustainable in the long term. A better solution would be to provide enough water for essential daily needs at no cost, while charging for excessive use such as filling swimming pools or watering large gardens.

In conclusion, I believe that clean water is indeed a basic human right, and every household should be guaranteed a free minimum supply. However, it is neither practical nor desirable for all water consumption to be completely free, and users should pay for non-essential or excessive use.

## 你可以直接套的模板骨架

### 引言模板

```text
[Topic] is often considered a basic human right. While I agree that everyone should have access to a basic supply of [topic], I do not believe that all of it should be provided entirely free of charge.
```

```text
On the one hand, there are strong reasons why governments should ensure that everyone has access to a basic amount of [topic]. It is essential for [功能1], [功能2], and [功能3]. Without it, people may suffer from [后果]. Therefore, providing a minimum level free of charge is both reasonable and humane.
```

### 主体段2：为什么不能全部免费

```text
On the other hand, making all use of [topic] free could create several problems. First, people may use it carelessly if they do not have to pay for it. Second, the system itself requires major investment in [成本1], [成本2], and [成本3]. A better approach would be to provide a basic amount for free while charging for excessive or non-essential use.
```

### 结论模板

```text
In conclusion, I believe that [topic] should be treated as a basic right, and a minimum supply should be free for all households. However, it would be impractical for all consumption to be free, so users should pay for unnecessary or excessive use.
```

## 这道题你千万别这么写

### 1. 立场极端

比如：

* “all water must be free”
* “people should always pay for water”

这种容易写偏，也不够现实。

### 2. 空话太多

比如只写：

* water is important
* government should help people
* people need water every day

这种太浅，分数上不去。

### 3. 没写“为什么全部免费不好”

题目很容易只写“水很重要”，但高分关键在于你要进一步解释：

* 为什么**基本用水**应免费
* 为什么**全部免费**不合理
