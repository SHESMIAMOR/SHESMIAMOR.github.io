# 一、先讲清楚：写作 7.5 看什么

雅思作文主要看四项：

1. **Task Response / Task Achievement**
   有没有正面回答题目，观点有没有展开。

2. **Coherence and Cohesion**
   结构清不清楚，段落是不是顺。

3. **Lexical Resource**
   词汇是否自然，不是越难越好。

   句型有变化，但更重要的是少出错。

# 二、Task 2 大作文万能高分模板

这是最重要的。
你想拿 7.5，Task 2 必须稳。

---

## 1）最万能的四段式结构

### 第1段：引言

### 第2段：理由1

### 第3段：理由2 / 让步段

### 第4段：结论

这套适合几乎所有题型。

---

## 2）引言高分模板

### 通用引言模板

```text
It is often argued that [题目改写]. While some people believe that [对立观点], I believe that [你的观点].
```

### 更自然一点的版本

```text
In recent years, there has been growing debate about whether [题目改写]. Although it is true that [对方观点], I would argue that [你的立场].
```

### 例子

题目：Some people think university education should be free for everyone.

你可以写：

```text
In recent years, there has been growing debate about whether university education should be free for all students. Although some people believe that individuals should pay for their own studies, I would argue that higher education ought to be made more affordable and accessible.
```

## 3）主体段模板

### 主体段开头模板

```text
The first reason is that ...
Another important point is that ...
One of the main arguments in favour of this view is that ...
```

### 展开模板

```text
This is because ...
In other words, ...
As a result, ...
For example, ...
```

### 一整段万能骨架

```text
The first reason is that [观点1]. This is because [解释]. In other words, [换句话说再解释一次]. As a result, [结果]. For example, [例子].
```

---

## 4）结论模板

### 通用结论

```text
In conclusion, although [让步], I believe that [重申观点] because [两个核心理由].
```

### 更高级一点

```text
In conclusion, while both sides of the argument have merit, I am convinced that [你的观点], mainly because [理由1] and [理由2].
```

# 三、Task 2 五大题型模板

---

## 1）Agree / Disagree 题

### 结构

* 引言：表明立场
* Body 1：理由1
* Body 2：理由2
* 结论

### 模板

```text
It is often argued that [题目改写]. I completely agree / partly agree / disagree with this view.

The first reason is that [理由1]. This is because [解释]. For example, [例子].

Another important reason is that [理由2]. As a result, [影响]. This shows that [回扣观点].

In conclusion, I believe that [重申观点] because [理由1] and [理由2].
```

---

## 2）Discuss Both Views and Give Your Opinion

### 结构

* 引言
* Body 1：第一种观点
* Body 2：第二种观点 + 你的立场
* 结论

### 模板

```text
People have different views about whether [题目改写]. While some argue that [观点1], others believe that [观点2]. This essay will discuss both views before explaining why I support [你的观点].

On the one hand, [观点1] is supported by the fact that [解释]. For instance, [例子].

On the other hand, proponents of [观点2] argue that [解释]. I agree with this view because [你的理由].

In conclusion, although [观点1] has some advantages, I believe that [观点2] is more convincing.
```

---

## 3）Advantages and Disadvantages

### 结构

* 引言
* 优点段
* 缺点段
* 结论（优缺点哪个更重要）

### 模板

```text
It is true that [题目改写]. This development has both benefits and drawbacks.

One major advantage is that [优点1]. This can lead to [结果]. For example, [例子].

However, there are also some disadvantages. The main one is that [缺点1]. As a consequence, [结果].

In conclusion, although [优点] is significant, I believe the drawbacks / benefits are more important overall.
```

---

## 4）Problem and Solution

### 结构

* 引言
* 问题段
* 解决段
* 结论

### 模板

```text
In recent years, [问题现象] has become a serious concern. This essay will examine the main causes of this issue and suggest some possible solutions.

One of the primary causes is that [原因1]. Another contributing factor is [原因2].

To address this problem, governments / individuals / schools should [解决办法1]. In addition, [解决办法2].

In conclusion, although [问题] is challenging, it can be tackled through [解决方案总结].
```

---

## 5）Two-part Question

这种题最容易丢分，因为很多人漏答。

### 模板

```text
The issue of [题目改写] has attracted considerable attention. This essay will first explain [问题1], and then discuss [问题2].

There are several reasons why [问题1答案]. The main one is that [解释]. For example, [例子].

As for [问题2], I believe that [回答]. This is because [解释].

In conclusion, [总结两个问题的回答].
```

# 六、你绝对不要做的事

想拿 7.5，这几个坑一定别踩。

### 1）不要背太多假高级词

比如乱用：

* indispensable
* paramount
* multifaceted
* ameliorate

能自然用可以，用不准就别用。

### 2）不要每句都写太长

7.5 不是靠一堆超长句，是靠：

* 长短句结合
* 基本无大错

### 3）不要空谈

每个主体段都要：

* 观点
* 解释
* 结果
* 例子

### 4）不要模板味太重

模板是骨架，不是全文照抄。
