# Common Listening Traps in This Section

## Trap 1 – Mentioned but rejected

Example:

`college grants`  
→ `they're all so small`

The option is **mentioned but rejected**.

---

## Trap 2 – Rejection expressions

Common phrases:

`turn me down`  
`I was mistaken`  
`not eligible`

These indicate **the option is wrong**.

---

## Trap 3 – Contrast signals

Example:

`I'm not very good`  
**but**  
`I'd really miss it`

In IELTS listening, the **real meaning usually comes after "but"**.

---
