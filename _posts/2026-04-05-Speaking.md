# to be snowed under
这个短语的意思是：
忙得不可开交；被大量工作/任务压得喘不过气。

它在雅思口语里很好用，因为它：
- 很地道
- 能自然表达“很忙”
- 比只说 very busy 更高级

---
## 最简单的高分造句
I’ve been absolutely snowed under with assignments lately, so I hardly have any time to relax.
意思是：
我最近作业多得忙不过来，所以几乎没有时间放松。

这句话为什么好用？
因为它不是只说：
I am very busy.
而是更具体、更自然：
snowed under with assignments
hardly have any time to relax

---
## 在雅思口语里怎么用才加分
这个短语最适合回答这些题：
Part 1
Are you busy these days?
Do you often have free time?
Do you like studying?
How do you balance work and rest?

你可以这样答：
Yes, definitely. I’ve been snowed under with coursework recently, especially because I’m preparing for exams and dealing with research tasks at the same time.
这句就比普通回答高级很多。

---
## 更自然一点的版本
To be honest, I’ve been snowed under recently because I’m juggling my studies, research, and IELTS preparation, so life feels a bit overwhelming at times.

这句很适合你本人，因为你现在确实在：
忙科研
忙雅思
忙申请 PhD

---
## 这里还有一个高分点：
juggle A, B, and C
意思是：
同时应付很多事。
如果你想拿更高分，不要只用短语，要会“展开”
低分说法
I’m snowed under.
太短，不够展开。
高分说法
I’ve been snowed under with academic work lately, mainly because I’m trying to prepare for IELTS while also working on my research project. As a result, I barely have any time for hobbies or rest.
这就很好，因为它有：
idiom：snowed under
原因：because...
结果：As a result...
这才像 7 分以上的口语表达。

---
## 你可以直接背的3个句子
1. I’ve been snowed under with academic work lately.
我最近学业忙得不可开交。

2. I’m usually snowed under before deadlines, especially when I have several tasks to finish at once.
截止日期前我通常忙得不可开交，尤其是同时要完成好几项任务的时候。

3. At the moment, I’m a bit snowed under because I’m preparing for IELTS and applying for PhD positions at the same time.
我现在有点忙不过来，因为我在同时准备雅思和申请博士。
这个短语怎么用才不显得生硬

## 最自然的搭配是：
snowed under with work
snowed under with assignments
snowed under with paperwork
snowed under with deadlines

不太建议你说特别奇怪的搭配，比如：
snowed under with happiness
snowed under with hobbies
这个短语通常和 工作、任务、压力 连用。

---
## 你在雅思里可以这样答题
如果考官问：
Are you busy these days?
你可以这样答：
Yes, absolutely. I’ve been snowed under with academic work lately because I’m preparing for IELTS and dealing with my research at the same time. It’s quite stressful, but I’m trying to manage everything step by step.
这个回答已经很像 7 分口语了。

---
## 最后提醒你一点
高分不是因为你用了 snowed under 这个短语本身，
而是因为你会这样用：
idiom + reason + result + personal detail

## 公式就是：
**I’ve been snowed under with ... because ... so ...**
